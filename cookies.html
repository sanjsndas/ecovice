Cookie Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This policy describes how Ecovice Planting uses cookies and similar technologies to manage and enhance your experience.

Understanding Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites. They enable websites to remember your preferences, authenticate logins, and collect analytics data, improving your interaction and website functionality.

We take a responsible approach to cookies, aiming to be transparent and respecting your privacy choices.

Types of Cookies Used

Essential Cookies

Necessary for core website functions like authentication and security. These cookies cannot be disabled by users without impacting the site.

Performance Cookies

Aggregate anonymous data used to analyze website traffic, identifying popular content and spotting usage patterns to enhance your experience.

Functional Cookies

Enable customized experiences based on your preferences including language and display settings.

Targeting Cookies

Used by us or third parties to deliver tailored advertisements and monitor marketing effectiveness.

How Cookies Improve Your Experience

  • Session management ensuring you stay logged in.
  • Remembering customization to avoid reconfiguring preferences.
  • Enabling security features.
  • Personalizing content and recommendations.
  • Gathering analytics to improve the website.

Third-Party Cookies & Services

We utilize third-party service providers such as Google Analytics to collect anonymized data.

Advertising partners may also use cookies to serve interest-based ads.

Third-party cookies are subject to their own privacy policies, accessible on their sites.

Managing and Controlling Cookies

You control your cookie preferences through browser settings or via the consent banner below at any time.

Disabling or deleting cookies may reduce website functionality or limit personalized features.

Cookie Consent Banner

Our consent banner requests your approval for non-essential cookies to comply with regulations and respect your privacy.

Your selection is stored for one year, but you can update choices anytime by clearing cookies or contacting us.

Lifecycle & Expiry of Cookies

Cookies have varying lifespans:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary and deleted when browser closes.
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain for set periods—ranging from days to years, depending on purpose.

Our cookies generally expire based on their function, such as security sessions or consent persistence.

Browser-Specific Behavior

Different browsers handle cookies uniquely, e.g., cookie blocking defaults, privacy modes, and third-party cookie restrictions.

This may affect how you experience our site and cookie functionality.

FAQs about Cookies

What are cookies?

Small data files stored on your device to improve website functionality and user experience.

Can I disable cookies?

Yes, via your browser settings or the consent options provided here, although this may reduce site usability.

Do you share cookie data?

Only aggregated anonymous data is shared with service providers for analytics and advertising under strict privacy terms.

Glossary of Terms

  • Cookie: A small file stored on a user’s device to maintain state across sessions.
  • First-party Cookie: Set by the website you are visiting.
  • Third-party Cookie: Set by a domain other than the website being visited, often for advertising.
  • Session Cookie: Exists temporarily during browser session.
  • Persistent Cookie: Remains after browser closes, expiring at a defined time.
  • Consent: User permission for data processing or cookie usage.

How to Change Your Cookie Preferences

Beyond the consent banner, you can adjust cookie settings in browser preferences or clear cookies manually using browser tools.

For more guidance, consult your browser’s help resources or contact us for assistance.

Opt-In vs Opt-Out Cookie Consent Models

Opt-In: Requires explicit user consent before setting cookies.

Opt-Out: Sets cookies by default but allows users to refuse or delete them after.

We implement an opt-in approach to respect your privacy and comply with regulations.
